Borderless Reimagined: teamLab's Digital Dreamscape Returns to Tokyo

teamLab has reopened its Borderless museum in Tokyo's Azabudai Hills, featuring new immersive artworks that blend technology, movement, and viewer interaction. The refreshed space continues the collective's mission to dissolve boundaries between art and experience.

A Walk Through Wonder: Inside the World of Ghibli Park

Step into the enchanting world of Studio Ghibli at Japan's newest theme park, where iconic scenes from beloved films like My Neighbor Totoro and Spirited Away come to life through immersive exhibits, life-sized sets, and magical landscapes.

Whispers in the Air: Yuko Nishikawa's Poetic Mobiles Dance in Tokyo

New York-based artist Yuko Nishikawa fills Calm & Punk Gallery's lofty space with ethereal mobiles inspired by the traditional Japanese poetic card game "Iroha Karuta." Delicate paper, pulp, and steel wire creations-responsive to ambient movement-dance alongside her acclaimed lighting sculptures in a dreamy dialogue.

Splashes of Spirit: Tokyo's Most Joyful Festival Returns This Summer

Held annually around August 15 at Tomioka Hachimangu Shrine, the Fukagawa Hachiman Festival is one of Edo's three great festivals. Known as the "water-splashing festival," participants drench mikoshi bearers in purifying water, creating a joyful and energetic celebration.

Big Dreams in Tiny Worlds: Tokyo's Miniature Museum Amazes All Ages

This Tokyo museum showcases intricately detailed miniatures of real and imaginary worlds, brought to life with motion, sound, and shifting day-night lighting. Highlights include a bustling Kansai International Airport and a steampunk-inspired Global Village from the early 20th century.

Where Goldfish Glow: Ginza's Art Aquarium Blends Nature and Neon

Located in Ginza, the Art Aquarium Museum turns goldfish into mesmerizing works of living art. Traditional Japanese aesthetics meet cutting-edge lighting and sound, offering a surreal and immersive visual experience.

Pen to Paper, Floor to Ceiling: Exploring Tokyo's Tower of Stationery

Located in Tokyo's upscale Ginza district, Ginza Itoya is a 12-story paradise for stationery lovers, offering everything from fine fountain pens to creative tools and lifestyle goods. Founded in 1904, the store blends tradition with innovation across its themed floors.

Green Gold: Where to Sip Japan's Finest Matcha

From Kyoto's serene tea houses to Tokyo's modern matcha bars, Japan offers a rich tapestry of experiences for matcha enthusiasts. In Uji, the birthplace of matcha, historic establishments like Tsuen Tea, founded in 1160, serve ceremonial-grade brews steeped in centuries of tradition.

Kyoto's Secret Garden: Rokujuan Teahouse and Its Edible Blossoms

Nestled in a century-old Kyoto residence, Rokujuan offers a tranquil escape with beautifully curated wagashi and artisanal teas. Its signature hanawarabi-flower-adorned mochi-pairs perfectly with matcha in a serene garden setting. A true taste of traditional Kyoto elegance.

Sail into the Magic: Why Tokyo DisneySea Is the Crown Jewel of Disney Parks

Exclusive to Japan, Tokyo DisneySea combines Disney magic with ocean-themed adventure across seven immersive ports, from Mediterranean Harbor to Port Discovery. With world-class rides like Journey to the Center of the Earth and Broadway-caliber shows, it offers a one-of-a-kind theme park experience.

A Stamped Stroll: Discover Kyoto's Gardens, One Mark at a Time

At Kyoto Botanical Garden, visitors can participate in a delightful stamp rally, collecting unique stamps at various points throughout the garden. This activity offers a fun and interactive way to explore the garden's diverse plant collections while creating a personalized souvenir.

Catch a Bite: Dining with Pikachu at Tokyo's Pokemon Cafe

Located in Tokyo's Nihonbashi district, the Pokemon Cafe offers fans an immersive dining experience with creatively themed dishes like the Pikachu Souffle Pancake and Eevee's Mixed Rice Plate. Guests can enjoy a variety of Pokemon-inspired meals and drinks, all served in a setting adorned with beloved characters and vibrant decor.
